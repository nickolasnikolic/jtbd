Jobs to be Done. (JTBD)

A job-to-be-done (JTBD) is a way of outlining and describing a service or product.

JTBD are famous, but not yet that famous.

A set of JBTD may be described about something as simple as a piece of chewing gum:

  • Provide some calories (but not too many) while not actually eating. (functional jtbd)
  • Provide a feeling of refreshment and taste. (emotional/personal jtbd)
  • Increase others' and your own confort in social circumstances with feeling of fresher breath for all participating. (emotional/social jtbd)

While this is a contrived example of a century old technology of chewing gum, it's intent is to point out what others describe as a very reasonable way of thinking about one's own or others' products.
