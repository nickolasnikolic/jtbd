Jobs to be Done. (JTBD)

A job-to-be-done (JTBD) is a way of outlining and describing a service or product.

JTBD are famous, but not yet that famous.

A set of JTBD may be described about something as simple as a piece of chewing gum:

  • Provide some calories (but not too many) while not actually eating, in order to better enjoy a snack. (functional jtbd)
  • Increase a feeling of refreshment and taste, in order to better enjoy a snack. (emotional/personal jtbd)
  • Increase others' and your own feeling of comfort in social circumstances with feeling of fresher breath for all participating, in order to better enjoy a snack. (emotional/social jtbd)

Did you notice the eating verb of the first in the list? That is a functional JTBD. The feeling of the last two? Those are emotional JTBD.

While this is a contrived example of a century old technology of chewing gum, it's intent is to point out what others describe as a very reasonable way of thinking about one's own or others' products.

Still interested? Why not try and add a few? You will need to add a product and then it's JTBD.
